03 / Profile

About

Hi, I’m Diego Cárdenas Mora, an Experience Designer born in Bogotá, Colombia, and currently based in the UK. I focus on creating and improving digital products and services through a user-centered design approach, applied research and close collaboration with interdisciplinary teams.

Driven by the desire to deepen my research practice and strengthen my methodological approach, I decided to move to the UK to pursue a Master’s degree in Human Computer Interaction at the University of St Andrews. Throughout this experience, I have refined my use of scientific methods in design, integrating qualitative and quantitative research to address problems from a critical, ethical, and human-centred perspective.

I’m always open to new collaborations where design can help make complex systems easier to understand and use.
